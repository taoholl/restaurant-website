Campus Meal Plan

Accepted at any location on campus, this plan can be set up with a $200 payment. You will save 5% on all purchases, except at Tim Hortons and Booster Juice. You can add money to this plan anytime as required. Any unused monies will be forwarded to the next semester. Ideal for full or part-time students living off-campus.

How does it work?

Our Meal Plans work on a declining balance system using your AC Card (student ID card). When you purchase your Meal Plan, food dollars are deposited into your account. Once you have chosen your Meal Plan, you simply present your Algonquin AC Card to the cashier at any of our seven locations. The appropriate food dollars from your selected Meal Plan will be deducted from your account. You may ask the cashier for a receipt after any purchase to view the balance available on your Meal Plan.
